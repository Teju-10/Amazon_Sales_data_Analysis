# Amazon Sales Analysis – Power BI Report

## Project Overview

This project presents a **Sales Analysis Dashboard** built using **Power BI** based on Amazon product sales data. The goal of this report is to analyze sales performance, product trends, and customer engagement through interactive and visually appealing insights.

---

##  Dataset Information

* **Source**: Amazon Sales Dataset (downloaded from external source)
* **Total Columns**: 89,082
* **Rows Included**:

  * Product Category
  * Product Description
  * Price ($)
  * Number of Reviews
  * Shipment
  * Order Date

---

##  Data Preparation

* Initially reviewed the dataset and found **no missing values**.
* Data was already **clean and well-structured**.
* Imported the dataset from **Excel into Power BI**.
* Used **Power Query Editor** for validation:

  * Verified all columns and data types.
  * Fixed formatting issue in the **Order Date column** (converted into proper date format).
* No major transformations were required as data quality was good.

---

##  Dashboard Development

Created a **single-page interactive dashboard** focusing on key business insights:

### Key Metrics (KPIs)

* **YTD Sales**: $2.18M
* **QTD Sales**: $811.09K
* **YTD Products Sold**: 28K
* **YTD Reviews**: 19M

---

### Visualizations Used

#### 1. Sales of the Month

* Line chart showing monthly sales trend.
* Helps identify **peak sales months and seasonal patterns**.

#### 2. Sales of the Week

* Bar chart representing weekly sales distribution.
* Useful for tracking **consistent growth over time**.

#### 3. Sales by Product Category

* Tabular visualization comparing:

  * YTD Sales
  * QTD Sales
  * % Contribution
* Highlights **top-performing categories** (e.g., Men Shoes, Camera).

#### 4. Top 5 Products by YTD Sales

* Horizontal bar chart showing best-selling products.
* Helps identify **high revenue-generating products**.

#### 5. Top 5 Products by Reviews

* Bar chart displaying most reviewed products.
* Indicates **customer engagement and popularity**.

---

## Key Insights

* Certain categories like **Men Shoes and Camera** contribute the highest sales.
* Sales show a **steady increase towards year-end**, indicating seasonal demand.
* High review counts correlate with **popular and trusted products**.
* Weekly sales trend shows **consistent growth pattern**.
